Plain‑English summary

SmackCheck is a social food app. We collect what we need to show you good food and remember your taste. We don't sell your data. Photos stay on your phone unless you post them. You can export or delete everything from Settings → Privacy.

  • Photos: analyzed on your device. Only uploaded when you choose to post.
  • Email + handle: required to make an account.
  • Location: optional. Used only for the map and tagging.
  • Analytics: anonymized, used to improve the app. You can opt out.

1. Information we collect

1.1 Information you give us

  • Account details: email, handle, display name, profile photo.
  • Content: smacks (photo + score + tasting note), comments, lists, follows.
  • Communications: messages you send to support@.

1.2 Information collected automatically

  • Device data: model, OS version, app version, language, time zone.
  • Usage data: screens visited, features used, crash reports (via FCM & in‑house telemetry).
  • Approximate location (city‑level), if you grant permission, for the map.

2. How we use your information

We use your data to:

  • Run the app — show you your feed, your map, your friends, your XP.
  • Score dishes (the AI model needs the photo to do its job).
  • Send notifications you opted into.
  • Improve product quality and AI accuracy (using aggregated, de‑identified data).
  • Detect abuse and enforce our Community Guidelines.

We do not sell your personal data, ever.

4. AI disclosure

SmackCheck uses machine‑learning models — primarily on‑device inference plus a server‑side flavor model — to recognize dishes and produce a smack score. Important:

  • Scores are estimates. They are not a substitute for human taste.
  • The model has known weaknesses on niche regional cuisines; we publish accuracy stats per cuisine in‑app.
  • You can override any AI output. Overrides are private unless you publish them.
  • We do not use your photos to train third‑party foundation models without your explicit opt‑in.

5. Retention

We retain your account data while your account is active. When you delete your account, content is removed from production within 30 days; backup snapshots roll off within 90 days. Photo binaries are deleted permanently.

6. Your rights

Under GDPR (EU) and CCPA (California), you have the right to access, correct, export, delete, and restrict processing of your personal data. To exercise any right:

  • In‑app: Settings → Privacy includes self‑serve export & delete.
  • By email: privacy@smackcheck.app — we respond within 14 days.

You can also lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

7. Minors

SmackCheck is for users 13 and older. We don't knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe we have, please contact privacy@smackcheck.app.

8. Changes to this policy

If we materially change this policy, we'll notify you in‑app at least 30 days before the change takes effect. Material changes require renewed acceptance.
